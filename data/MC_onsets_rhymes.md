Eight chongniu rhymes:

1. **支 Zhī** (支, 紙, 寘): Notated as `-je` (III) vs. `-jie` (IV).
2. **脂 Zhī** (脂, 旨, 至): Notated as `-ij` (III) vs. `-jij` (IV).
3. **宵 Xiāo** (宵, 小, 笑): Notated as `-jew` (III) vs. `-jiew` (IV).
4. **真 Zhēn** (真, 軫, 震, 質): Notated as `-in` (III) vs. `-jin` (IV) / -it (III) vs. -jit (IV)
5. **諄 Zhūn** (諄, 準, 稕, 術): A group split from 真, notated as `-win` (III) vs. `-jwin` (IV) / -wit (III) vs. -jwit (IV)
6. **仙 Xiān** (仙, 獮, 線, 薛): Notated as `-jen` (III) vs. `-jien` (IV) / -jet (III) vs. -jiet (IV) 
7. **侵 Qīn** (侵, 寢, 沁, 緝): Notated as `-im` (III) vs. `-jim` (IV) / -ip (III) vs. -jip (IV)
8. **鹽 Yán** (鹽, 琰, 豔, 葉): Notated as `-jem` (III) vs. `-jiem` (IV) / -jep (III) vs. -jiep (IV)
